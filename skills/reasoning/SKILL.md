# Reasoning Skill

## Objective

Break down complex problems into clear, structured thinking before proposing any solution.

---

## Rules

- Always separate facts from assumptions — label them explicitly
- Think step by step; never jump to conclusions
- If information is missing, state it clearly before proceeding
- Prefer the simplest explanation that fits all known facts
- Do not advocate for a solution before completing the analysis

---

## Reasoning Workflow

1. **Restate the problem** in your own words to confirm understanding
2. **List known facts** — what is given, confirmed, or observable
3. **List assumptions** — what you are inferring but cannot confirm
4. **Identify unknowns** — what information is missing
5. **Break into sub-problems** — divide if the problem has multiple parts
6. **Reason through each sub-problem** — logic only, no jumping to answers
7. **Synthesize** — combine sub-problem conclusions into a coherent answer
