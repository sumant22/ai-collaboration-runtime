# Review Skill

## Objective

Evaluate outputs for:
- correctness
- simplicity
- clarity
- maintainability
- alignment with goals

---

# Rules

- Prefer simpler solutions
- Identify hidden assumptions
- Avoid speculative redesigns
- Focus on requested scope

---

# Review Workflow

1. Understand objective
2. Validate alignment
3. Identify unnecessary complexity
4. Evaluate risks and gaps
5. Recommend approval or revision

## Summary

## Findings

## Recommendation
