# Planning Skill

## Objective

Produce a clear, actionable, sequenced plan from a goal or problem statement.

---

## Rules

- Every plan step must be concrete and assignable — no vague steps like "figure out X"
- Identify dependencies between steps explicitly
- Always include a risk or blocker section
- Do not over-plan — stop at the level of detail needed to take action
- If a step requires a decision, mark it as a decision point

---

## Planning Workflow

1. **Clarify the goal** — what does success look like exactly?
2. **Identify constraints** — time, resources, dependencies, non-negotiables
3. **List major phases** — high-level stages to reach the goal
4. **Break phases into steps** — concrete, actionable tasks
5. **Sequence steps** — order them, mark dependencies
6. **Identify risks and blockers** — what could go wrong at each phase?
7. **Define success criteria** — how will you know each step is done?
